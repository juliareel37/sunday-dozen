Frequently Asked Questions

Are your cookies celiac safe?

Yes! My cookies are safe for those with celiac disease. The kitchen I work in is 100% free of gluten, so cross contamination is not a risk.

Do you deliver?

Yes! I can deliver within Ann Arbor, MI for a $5 delivery fee. I most commonly deliver on the University of Michigan campus, but can deliver anywhere with an Ann Arbor address.

Do you take bulk orders?

Yes! I am more than happy to take orders for corporate events, bridal/baby showers, weddings, and more. If you are looking to place a bulk order, please send us a message. If you place a bulk order and live outside of Ann Arbor, MI, we can discuss the possibility of delivery or different pickup options.

Do you make cakes or cupcakes?

At this time, no I do not. I only make cookies at the moment but I am hoping to expand into more baked goods in the future!
