ABOUT US

Hi, I'm Sophie! Sunday Dozen began in my home kitchen after I set out to make gluten-free treats that are just as good as the classics. I fell in love with the process of experimenting, sharing with friends, and turning cozy Sunday bakes into something the neighborhood could enjoy.

I bake in small batches with high quality, 100% gluten-free ingredients. Every box is made with care, and I love helping you celebrate the little moments.
